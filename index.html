About Siip Abacus

Our mission is to empower students through structured abacus training, helping them improve calculation speed, focus, and confidence for academic success

Why Choose Our Institute?

Established in 2003, SIIP Abacus aims to revolutionize education by offering structured abacus training that goes beyond textbooks, enhancing speed, accuracy, and cognitive skills in children..

Expert Faculty

Carefully designed abacus modules, continuous practice drills, and skill-based exercises to ensure strong mental math proficiency.
